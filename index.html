The purpose of building Wave was to experiment in using passive-listening as meditatation technique. Inspired by years of field recording, users listen to repetitious wave-like sounds to help dive within.

SHAMER

SHAMER is an app created to bring fierce accountability to goals. Users can input goals and their due dates, and failing to reach those goals leads to both monetary and social loss

Attention

Inspired by Aldous Huxley's novel Island, Attention is a simple app that reminds you to be present by sending you the message "Here and Now, Boys!" at a user-selectable frequency
